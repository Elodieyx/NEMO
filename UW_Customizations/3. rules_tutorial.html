NanoFab Rules Tutorial

All lab users must have successfully completed the Workplace Hazardous Materials Information System (WHMIS) course, available at the University of Waterloo Safety Office website.

To pass the NanoFab Rules Tutorial and gain access to the G2N Lab tools, lab users must:

  1. Read and understand the the Access to G2N Facilities: COVID-19 Precautions presentation.
  2. Sign the acknowledgment form at the last slide of the presentation, confirming your understanding of the information and agreement to comply with the rules for safe behavior in G2N facilities.
  3. Email the acknowledgement form to Richard Barber, the G2N Lab Manager.
    • Richard Barber: rabarber@uwaterloo.ca
    • Email subject: "Acknowledgment: COVID-19 Requirements for G2N"
    • Include your WatIAM username in the email.
  4. When you receive a reply that your training has been completed, you will then have access to the G2N Lab tools.

For questions or concerns, you may contact Richard Barber, rabarber@uwaterloo.ca.
